Hotel Name:The Bungalow on the Beach, Tamil Nadu - CPAI (Breakfast Only)

This 18th century Danish colonial house today is one of the best heritage hotels in Tamil Nadu. With just 8 spacious well-equipped rooms with breathtaking views, this property is ideal for honeymooners and leisure vacationers post lockdown with full social distancing guidelines being followed by the management, Neemrana Group. The property has a 17th century Danish fort on the right; a 14th century Pandya temple bounds it from the left.
